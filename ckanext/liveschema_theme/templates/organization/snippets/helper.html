This section provides an observatory of the ontology and vocabulary catalogs ecosystem.
This collection of catalogs is continuously updated, according to admins initiative or following suggestions by users.

At the current state LiveSchema hosts schemas (i.e. vocabularies) catalogs only.
The extension of LiveSchema with other kinds of data catalogs is part of future work.

Most of the catalogs are free to browse, other catalogs need subscription credentials.

Users (according to their level of authorization) can create, manage and publish datasets within catalogs.

If you want to suggest a new catalog, please Contact us.
