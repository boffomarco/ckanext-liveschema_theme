Support the process of managing diversity in reference knowledge resources.
Ontology repository to handle vocabularies.
LiveSchema offers thoroughly developed state-of-the-art standard solutions in this field such as browse, search, access, metadata exploration, and so on. Moreover LiveSchema will also supply developers with new and more advanced services such as the aggregation of ontologies from different sources, an auto-updating procedure, a visualization mechanism, and a process for the production of new knowledge.
Thanks to this set of tools offered, LiveSchema aim to help the analysis and re-use of existing vocabularies in order to obtain newly generated ontologies that will best suit the personal needs of the users.
